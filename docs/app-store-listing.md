## Description (EN)

**PokerIQ** — the smartest way to train your poker math.

Stop guessing. Start calculating. PokerIQ turns poker probability training into an addictive quiz game. Whether you're a beginner learning what "outs" means or an intermediate player fine-tuning pot odds decisions, PokerIQ has you covered.

**LEARN BY DOING**
• 9 structured chapters from basic outs counting to advanced EV decisions
• Every question comes with a detailed explanation card
• Smart difficulty scaling based on your performance

**PRACTICE WITHOUT LIMITS**
• Quick Practice mode with infinite AI-generated questions
• 4 question types: Outs, Equity, Pot Odds, Preflop Action
• Mistake Review system that regenerates questions on your weak spots

**COMPETE WEEKLY**
• 10-question timed challenges refreshed every week
• Global leaderboard — see how you stack up
• Track your accuracy trend over time

**PROBABILITY CALCULATOR**
• Full-featured odds calculator for 2/6/9-player tables
• Intuitive card picker with visual suit selection
• Win rate, outs, hit probability — all in one tap

**TRACK YOUR PROGRESS**
• Weekly accuracy trend charts
• Per-category breakdown (Outs / Equity / Odds / Preflop)
• Customizable poker persona avatars

PokerIQ is a pure educational tool. It does NOT involve real-money gambling or betting of any kind.
